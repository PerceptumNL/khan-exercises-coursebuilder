Given the ROW \times COL matrix A = writeMatrix(MATRIX), determine A^T.

matrixToText(transMatrix(MATRIX))

To find A^T we take the transpose of A.

To transpose a matrix, we switch the rows and columns of the original matrix.

Since A is a ROW \times COL matrix, A^T is a COL \times ROW matrix.

That means that:
writeMatRows(MATRIX)

When we put the columns together, we get A^T.

A^T = writeMatrix(transMatrix(MATRIX))
